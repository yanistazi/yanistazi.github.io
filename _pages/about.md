<em>(Updated May 2026)</em>

Hi, I’m **Yanis Tazi** — Founding Principal ML Engineer and Head of Compliance at **AminoChain**.

I build production-grade, secure AI systems for biomedical research, with a focus on:
- **Agentic AI for scientific workflows**
- **GenAI and multimodal foundation models**
- **AI safety, governance, and compliance-by-design**

Before AminoChain, I was a Senior Data Scientist at **Johnson & Johnson**, where I worked on GenAI and multimodal AI initiatives for oncology and translational research.

## What I do

I work at the intersection of:
- machine learning research,
- real-world healthcare deployment,
- and operational/compliance rigor.

My goal is to turn advanced ML into products that are not only accurate and useful, but also auditable, secure, and trusted by clinical and enterprise users.

## Background

I completed my PhD in the [Tri-Institutional CBM Program (Cornell, MSKCC, Rockefeller)](https://compbio.triiprograms.org/), with research across computational medicine, neuroscience, and AI. I also completed an [MBA at NYU Stern](https://www.stern.nyu.edu/programs-admissions/executive-mba-nyc/program-details/class-profile), focused on technology and operations leadership.

My prior work spans:
- computational oncology,
- neural and behavioral modeling,
- drug discovery,
- and quantitative modeling in industry.

Across these settings, I’ve developed ML systems on genomic, imaging, and clinical data to support disease characterization, prognosis, and decision support.

*Updated: January 2026*

Hi, I’m **Yanis Tazi** — a **Founding Principal ML Engineer and Head of Compliance at AminoChain** focused on production-grade AI systems for biomedical research and healthcare.

## What I do

I build and lead end-to-end AI initiatives at the intersection of:

- **Agentic AI systems** for healthcare workflows
- **Multimodal machine learning** for clinical decision support
- **AI security, governance, and compliance** for safe deployment

Previously, I worked as a **Senior Data Scientist at Johnson & Johnson**, where I contributed to GenAI and multimodal AI efforts in oncology and translational research.

## Research & industry background

My work spans computational medicine, neuroscience, and applied machine learning. Across academia and industry, I’ve developed models for:

- Cancer risk stratification and progression modeling
- Facial movement modeling and representation learning
- Drug discovery with graph neural networks
- Neural and behavioral data analysis in computational neuroscience

I enjoy translating research into practical tools that clinicians, scientists, and product teams can trust and use.

## Education

- **MBA**, NYU Stern School of Business (Management of Technology & Operations)
- Engineering and mathematics training across MINES ParisTech, Université Paris Dauphine, NUS, and UQAM

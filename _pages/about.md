*Updated May 2026*

Hi, I’m **Yanis Tazi, PhD, MBA** — Founding Principal ML Engineer and Head of Compliance at **AminoChain**.

I design and deploy secure, production-grade AI systems for biomedical research, with a focus on:

- Agentic AI for scientific and clinical workflows
- GenAI and multimodal AI in translational medicine
- AI governance, model safety, and compliance-by-design

Before AminoChain, I worked as a Senior Data Scientist at Johnson & Johnson, where I focused on GenAI and multimodal AI for oncology use cases.

---

## What I work on

My work sits at the intersection of **AI engineering**, **computational medicine**, and **clinical impact**:

- Building AI systems that transform multimodal biomedical data (genomics, imaging, EHR-like signals) into clinically useful insights
- Developing interpretable ML approaches for disease stratification and progression modeling
- Bringing security and compliance principles into end-to-end ML product development

I’m especially interested in moving from research prototypes to systems that are trustworthy, auditable, and usable in real-world healthcare settings.

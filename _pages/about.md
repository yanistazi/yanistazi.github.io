<em>(Updated May 2026)</em>

Hi, I'm Yanis.

I’m a Founding Principal ML Engineer at AminoChain, where I build secure, agentic AI systems for biomedical research. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, healthcare, and trustworthy AI operations.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Data Scientist at Johnson & Johnson on GenAI and multimodal AI for cancer research, and held research/industry roles spanning Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, Roivant Sciences, and quantitative modeling in finance.

I completed a PhD in the Tri-Institutional Program (Cornell, MSKCC, Rockefeller) focused on computational biology/medicine and neuroscience, and an MBA at NYU Stern focused on management of technology and operations.

### What I work on

- Agentic AI for biomedical and clinical research workflows
- Multimodal ML (genomics, imaging, clinical and behavioral data)
- AI safety, security, and compliance in regulated settings
- Translating research prototypes into production systems

### Research themes

My research has included disease-risk modeling in oncology, interpretable deep learning for facial/behavioral phenotyping, and graph neural networks for drug discovery.

### Beyond work

I enjoy traveling, hiking, fitness, football (soccer), and discovering new cities.

## Featured Projects

### 1) AML Risk Stratification & Clinical Decision Support
- **Project:** Multi-state Markov and molecular risk modeling for AML progression and outcomes.
- **Impact:** Contributed to a unified framework for AML classification/risk stratification and an open-access decision support direction.
- **Publication:** [Unified classification and risk-stratification in Acute Myeloid Leukemia (Nature Communications, 2022)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-022-32103-8)
- **Code:** [papaemmelab/Tazi_NatureC_AML](https://github.com/papaemmelab/Tazi_NatureC_AML)

### 2) Interpretable Functional Specialization in Neural Networks
- **Project:** Studied emergence of specialized representations for identity vs expression processing.
- **Publication:** [ICML CompBio Workshop (2023)](https://icml-compbio.github.io/2023/papers/WCBICML2023_paper40.pdf)
- **Code:** [yanistazi/Identity_Expression_Specialization](https://github.com/yanistazi/Identity_Expression_Specialization)

### 3) Self-Supervised Person-Specific Models (PSM)
- **Project:** Objective characterization of individual facial dynamics with self-supervised learning.
- **Publication:** [arXiv:2211.08279](https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.08279)
- **Code:** [yanistazi/PSM_release](https://github.com/yanistazi/PSM_release)

## Featured Work

### 1) AML Molecular Risk Platform (Clinical Decision Support)
- **What it is:** A translational ML and statistical modeling framework used to classify all AML patients into molecular subtypes and improve risk stratification.
- **Impact:** Published in *Nature Communications* with a practical web app for clinician/researcher exploration.
- **Links:**
  - Project/App: [aml-risk-model.com](https://www.aml-risk-model.com/)
  - Paper: [Unified classification and risk-stratification in AML (Nature Communications, 2022)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-022-32103-8)
  - Code: [Tazi_NatureC_AML](https://github.com/papaemmelab/Tazi_NatureC_AML)

### 2) Interpretable Functional Specialization in Neural Networks
- **What it is:** A computational neuroscience + deep learning framework that studies how expression vs. identity pathways can emerge in neural systems.
- **Impact:** Connects representation learning with interpretable functional specialization hypotheses inspired by brain systems.
- **Links:**
  - Code: [Identity_Expression_Specialization](https://github.com/yanistazi/Identity_Expression_Specialization)
  - Paper: [ICML CompBio Workshop 2023](https://icml-compbio.github.io/2023/papers/WCBICML2023_paper40.pdf)

### 3) Self-Supervised Person-Specific Models for Facial Movement
- **What it is:** Self-supervised individual-level models to characterize facial motion while reducing identity confounding.
- **Impact:** Demonstrates clinically relevant and interpretable embedding strategies for human movement/behavior quantification.
- **Links:**
  - Code: [PSM_release](https://github.com/yanistazi/PSM_release)
  - Paper: [arXiv:2211.08279](https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.08279)

### 4) Graph Neural Networks for Drug Discovery
- **What it is:** GNN + attention modeling for protein-protein complex scoring.
- **Impact:** Advanced structure-aware modeling workflows for early-stage discovery and ranking of candidate interactions.
- **Links:**
  - Code: [Graph_Neural_Net_Protein-Protein-Complexes](https://github.com/yanistazi/Graph_Neural_Net_Protein-Protein-Complexes)

### 5) Oncology Imaging ML (Multiple Instance Learning)
- **What it is:** Multiple-instance learning with CNN encoders and sequence modeling for lesion/metastasis-level reasoning.
- **Impact:** Showcases end-to-end pipelines for weakly supervised medical imaging tasks.
- **Links:**
  - Code: [MIL_CNN_RNN_Imaging](https://github.com/yanistazi/MIL_CNN_RNN_Imaging)
